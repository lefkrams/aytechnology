Frequently Asked Questions

What is cloud telephony?

Cloud telephony is a modern phone system that operates through the internet instead of traditional phone hardware. It allows businesses to manage calls, extensions and communication from anywhere.

What are the benefits of VoIP for businesses?

VoIP helps businesses reduce phone costs, improve flexibility, support remote work and access advanced calling features.

Can cloud phone systems support remote teams?

Yes. Cloud telephony allows employees to make and receive business calls from offices, homes or mobile devices.

Can AYTechnology help migrate our existing phone system?

Yes. AYTechnology can help businesses transition from traditional phone systems to modern cloud-based communication solutions.
